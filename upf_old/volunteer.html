Volunteer With Us


Volunteering holds many benefits. It can help you to develop new skills build confidence meet new people create career opportunities and even provides you peace of mind and happiness that help to improve mental and physical fitness.

Every soul needs its dose to keep it happy and peaceful. Volunteering with us will provide you opportunity to connect with the orphans and special cause.

If you have any kind of skill talent that can help us to improve our work. Kindly forward your CV.

Terms & Condition for membership


(1) No salary shall be paid to any of the members/volunteers in any way shape or form.

(2) United Peace Foundation is not responsible for member’s/volunteer’s personal character either good or bad.

(3) United Peace Foundation is not responsible for any member/volunteer if he/she violates any law of the country or found guilty in any anti-social/national activities.

(4) United Peace Foundation is not responsible if member/volunteer gets into trouble when working with other social welfare organization or political party.

(5) United Peace Foundation will not provide any kind of help to member/volunteer in any social or personal problem unless it is directly related to assigned work by United Peace Foundation.

(6) It is not valid for a member/volunteer to assign himself/herself a designation/position in United Peace Foundation or in central office.

(7) Member/volunteer cannot use name/fame for his personal benefits whatsoever.

(8) United Peace Foundation has full authority to cancel membership at any time without prior notice and without any valid reason/cause.

United Peace Foundation is a non-profit organization established to help and uplift the most vulnerable, orphans, destitute and widows regardless of faith race or creed.
