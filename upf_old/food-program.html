Food programme


Dietary assistance or food parcel is a lifeline for the families who have no bread earner and old age destitute who are not able to work or earn money to get foods.



UPF provide necessary food parcels to widow headed family, destitute and orphans which is not possible without your generosity and kindness.

United Peace Foundation is a non-profit organization established to help and uplift the most vulnerable, orphans, destitute and widows regardless of faith race or creed.
